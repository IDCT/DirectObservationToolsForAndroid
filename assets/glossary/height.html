Height

Number of stories or floors above ground, including the ground floor and floors above. AIso includes storage and mechanical plant levels only where their area is greater than 30% of the building plan area, but does not include basement levels. If the building is stepped in height, then record the highest part.

An alternative way of describing the building height is by means of height categories: low-rise (1 to 3 stories), mid-rise (4 to 7 stories), high-rise or tall (8 to 14 stories), very tall (15 to 36 stories), skyscrapers (37 to 110 stories), and super-tall (greater than 110 stories or height greater than 300 metres).
