Reinforced concrete slabs, flat slabs or plates (FC1)

Reinforced concrete slabs are supported by beams or load-bearing walls. Flat slabs are supported by columns with capitals, and flat plates are supported by columns without capitals. Slabs or plates can be post-tensioned.
