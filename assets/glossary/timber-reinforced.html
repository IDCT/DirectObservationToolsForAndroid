Wood-reinforced

Masonry walls that are reinforced against earthquake forces by horizontal and vertical wood members. The wood reinforcement might consist of wood bond beams at eaves level to tie walls together or may be vertical posts attached to walls to reduce the amount of earthquake damage. Taq, bhata, hatil and dhajji dewari construction are examples of wood-reinforced construction.
