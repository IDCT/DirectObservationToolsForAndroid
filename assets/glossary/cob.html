Cob

Earthen construction using clay-based soil usually mixed with sand, straw and water. The material is first kneaded and then shaped into large balls, which are stacked on top of one another to form monolithic walls. A layer is left to dry before adding the next layer to build up walls. It is not rammed after placement, as with Rammed Earth. The earthern mixture contains fibres, usually straw, grass or twigs.
