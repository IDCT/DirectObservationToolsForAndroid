Dressed stone [STDRE]

Square or rectangular-shaped prismatic stone blocks in bonded courses with narrow joints, with or without mortar. Usually found in areas where calcareous stones and tuffs (rocks formed from volcanic ash), which are relatively easy to shape, are available.
