Pitched with gable ends [RSH2]

The roof is pitched (sloped) on two sides up to a central ridge. At each end of the roof, i.e. the other two sides, there is a vertical gable. Gable is a triangular wall between the roof wedges.

Pitched roof with gable walls (shown in white colour), Chile (S. Brzev)
